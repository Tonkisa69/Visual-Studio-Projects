User Roles and Permissions

I. Students

Students are the primary users of the platform who submit homework assignments and view their grades.

What Students Cannot Do:

  • Create or modify homework assignments.
  • View grades or submissions of other students.
  • Perform administrative tasks such as user management or grade assignments.
